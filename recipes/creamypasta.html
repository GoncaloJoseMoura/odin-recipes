Creamy tomato pasta

Description

In this recipe, we'll whip up a simple yet flavorful creamy tomato pasta dish that's bursting with sun-dried tomato goodness. The pasta is coated in a rich and creamy tomato sauce, enhanced by the sweetness and tanginess of sun-dried tomatoes, and finished with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese for a touch of umami. This is a quick and easy meal that's perfect for a weeknight dinner or a casual get-together.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Tomato sauce
  • Heavy cream
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook pasta al dente.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet in medium heat.
  3. Saut√© the garlic.
  4. Add tomato paste, tomato sauce, cream, italian seasoning and stir until smooth.
  5. Let it on until the sauce has thickened.
  6. Add a pinch of sugar.
  7. Add the drained pasta.
  8. Top it with parmesan cheese.
